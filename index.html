Welcome to PinchDraw!

How to Use:

  1. Click Start Camera and allow camera access when prompted
  2. Keep your hand visible to the camera
  3. Pinch your thumb and index finger together to start drawing
  4. Open your hand to stop drawing
  5. Adjust brush color, size, and sensitivity in the controls below

Features:

  • Real-time hand tracking with skeleton overlay
  • Pinch-to-draw gesture control
  • Customizable brush settings
